Video generation can take up to 30s

To create a live photo:

  1. Download from the appstore intoLive
  2. Download this video and save in into your photo library
  3. Open intoLive
  4. Select your video from the list
  5. Click make in the top-right corner
  6. Select no repeat
  7. Click save live photo
  8. Open the photo app again
  9. Click on the newly created photo
  10. Set it as a wallpaper
  11. Make sure that the Live Photo option is on
